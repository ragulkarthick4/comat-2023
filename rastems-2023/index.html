About Us

NEW RAINS is an emerging scientific and professional organization from India, established in the year 2023. NEW RAINS aims to connect the Research Scholars, Scientists, Academicians and Professionals from Industry. NEW RAINS will serve the International Research and Academic community through dissemination of knowledge on recent advances in various fields of Science, Engineering, Medical science, Social science, Management etc. among the students, researchers, academicians and professionals through various activities including publications and conferences.

About AASTU

Addis Ababa Science and Technology University (AASTU) was established in 2011. AASTU is the first University to be established as a specialized institution for science and technology in the history of Ethiopia.

About KVCET

Karpaga Vinayaga College of Engineering and Technology was established in the year 2001, Approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.

About the Conference

The International conference on Recent advances in Science Technology, Engineering, Management and Social science is organised in association with ADDIS ABABA SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY UNIVERSITY, Biotechnology and Bioprocess center of excellence, Ethiopia. KARPAGAVINAYAGA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY, Chengalpattu. This conference focuses on the recent developments in Computer Science and Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Management and English. NEW RAINS organises this conference at International level in order to provide a platform to scientists, research scholars and academicians to share their research findings with the peers in the world. The conference will be indexed. Highly reputed international scientists and top academicians from international universities will deliver invited talks. All papers submitted to the conference will be peer reviewed by the technical committee before acceptance. E-proceedings and hard copies with ISBN will be published during the conference.

PUBLICATION

All submitted papers will be reviewed by the technical committee of the conference (including similarity index check). The Proceedings containing the abstracts of all accepted papers will be released on the first day of the conference. Furthermore, all the selected papers will be peer-reviewed by the experts of the specialized area and recommended for journal review. At least one of the authors of the selected paper should register and present the paper in the respective conference venue/ online mode. All the selected Abstracts will be published as in the Conference proceedings with ISBN number during the conference. All the selected peer reviewed full papers will be published in SCI/ SCIE/ ESCI/ SCOPUS/ Web of Science (UGC CARE List-II) and UGC CARE List-I journals (upon payment of Article Processing Charge, as per journal norms).

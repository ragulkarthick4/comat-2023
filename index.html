NEW RAINS is an emerging scientific and professional organization from India, established in the year 2023. NEW RAINS aims to connect the Scholars, Scientists, Academicians and Professionals from Industry. NEW RAINS will serve the International Research and Academic community through dissemination of knowledge on recent advances in various fields of Science, Engineering, Medical science, Social science, Management etc. among the students, researchers, academicians and professionals through various activities including publications and conferences.

Latest Conferences

NEW RAINS organises conferences at International level in order to provide a platform to scientists, research scholars and academicians to share their research findings with the peers in the world. All such conferences will be indexed. Highly reputed international scientists and top academicians from international universities will deliver invited talks. All papers submitted for the conferences will be peer reviewed before acceptance. Proceedings with ISBN will be brought out after every conference.
